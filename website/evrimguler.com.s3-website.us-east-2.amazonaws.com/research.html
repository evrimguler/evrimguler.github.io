Research

In the doctoral program, I have been doing academic studies on Software-Defined Networking(SDN), Network Virtualization, and Flexi-Grid Optical Networks under the supervising of Dr. Xiaojun Cao. I had studied on localization and tracking in Wireless Sensor Networks, Smart Grid communications and demand-side price optimization, mobile applications development, and introducing programming to non-CS majors to understand the theory and implementation of the research fields. To concentrate on these active areas has provided me with an opportunity to gain in-depth understanding of the topics of the field. I have also developed my research skills to carry out advanced work within the discipline.
